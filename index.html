The Sanskaram College of Veterinary and Animal Sciences (COVAS) is committed to providing the best education and facilities to its students. Our infrastructure includes modern audio-visual laboratories, air-conditioned residential accommodation for faculty, and separate hostels for boys and girls with a variety of food options.


Sanskaram COVAS is proud to house the Sanskaram IVF Center, Genetics Lab & Semen Center, established in 2022. It is India's second lab of its kind, offering state-of-the-art facilities and advanced assisted breeding techniques for dairy genetic improvement. The lab's main objective is to find ways for improving quality semen, sexed semen, and IVF to increase milk yield in the next generation. The lab has signed important cooperation agreements with countries like Bangladesh and Brazil, while delegations from Russia have also visited and shown interest in the lab's possibilities.

International Institute of Veterinary Education and Research (IIVER) was established in the year 2011 under the umbrella of Jan Shakti Charitable Trust, Rohtak. The main objective of the trust is to provide help in the establishment or maintenance of educational institutes including schools and colleges which can either be for general or technical education including medicine, veterinary, commerce, agriculture, literature, philosophy, science including domestic science or arts, physical education etc. The trust shall also help in promotion, encouragement and advancement of education in art and artistic efforts in all its branches including imparting of knowledge in art, allied or abstract music, painting, drama, dancing, photography, sculpture and of any other kind.

The college is situated at by-pass, National Highway 10, Bahu Akbarpur, Rohtak, 10 kms from Rohtak city. The location is ideally suited as Delhi is only 75 kms away and the area falls under National Capital Region and is the home of world renowned Murrah buffaloes also called as Black Gold.
